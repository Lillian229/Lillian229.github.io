### 具体操作

eg.添加obsidian动作——将划选的文字指定为彩色
1. 找到要使用Quicker的软件

![](https://hcx-blog-images.oss-cn-chengdu.aliyuncs.com/images/Pastedimage20211128203911.png)

---

2. 搜索/选择要使用的功能，点击进去

![](https://hcx-blog-images.oss-cn-chengdu.aliyuncs.com/images/Pastedimage20211128204228.png)

---

3. 点击“复制到剪贴板”

![](https://hcx-blog-images.oss-cn-chengdu.aliyuncs.com/images/Pastedimage20211128204308.png)

---

4. 在obsidian（相应软件）的空白处按鼠标中间键，点击➕ 号

![](https://hcx-blog-images.oss-cn-chengdu.aliyuncs.com/images/Pastedimage20211128204945.png)

---

5. 点击“粘贴分享的动作”

![](https://hcx-blog-images.oss-cn-chengdu.aliyuncs.com/images/Pastedimage20211128205100.png)

---

6. 点击“安装”，即可安装

![](https://hcx-blog-images.oss-cn-chengdu.aliyuncs.com/images/Pastedimage20211128205144.png)

---

7.  安装成功后使用，点击鼠标中间键，选择要使用的功能即可

![](https://hcx-blog-images.oss-cn-chengdu.aliyuncs.com/images/Pastedimage20211128205303.png)

---
